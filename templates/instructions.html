Each panel needs an image, a dialog for the character in the image, three response options, and corresponding redirects for each of the response options.

The redirect sends it from the current panel to the panel specified. If the panel is not in range of any current panels, then the game will redirect you to an end screen.

The text options can all redirect to the same place, although many players will like the feeling of variety with their choices.
